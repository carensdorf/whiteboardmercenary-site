Why Interviewing Breaks Down

Even high-performing teams fall short when interviews are treated like art instead of science. Here's where the cracks usually show:

Uncalibrated Loops

Without a system, interview loops become guesswork. Questions aren't leveled, core competencies get missed, and modern skills, like GenAI fluency, go unassessed.

Inconsistent Signal Capture

Notes are messy. Scores are subjective. Debriefs devolve into opinions instead of decisions. You can't optimize interviewing without clean, consistent data.

Untrained Interviewers

Interviewing isn't an instinct, it's a skill. Most engineers are never taught how to evaluate candidates objectively or align on what "good" looks like. The result? Chaos disguised as culture fit.
